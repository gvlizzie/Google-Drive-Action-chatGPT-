Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-10

This tool lets you export files from your Google Drive and gives you a download link in ChatGPT.

What we store

  • Your Google sign-in permission (token) so we can read your files when you ask.
  • Basic file info (name/type) to list and export files.
  • Exported files only for a few minutes to create a one-time download link.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not keep exported files after the link is used or expires.

Your choices

  • Stop access anytime: Google Account permissions.
  • Ask us to delete stored tokens/logs: you@example.com
